Anonymous 26-Jul-20 04:57 PM
You can decompose any one-qubit operator into a Pauli vector (a linear combination of the Pauli matrices) as shown here: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pauli_matrices#Pauli_vector. Also, entangled states cannot be decomposed into a tensor product of independent qubit states or superpositions (e.g. you can’t factor a Bell state into A|0> x B|1>).
