Anonymous 20-Jul-20 10:48 PM
Technically the state is |+> tensorproduct |1>, where |+> = 1/sqrt(2)|0> + 1/sqrt(2)|1>. So the control qubit is |+> and the target qubit is |1>. We know the effect of CNOT when the control qubit is |0> or |1>. We do not know what is the effect of CNOT when the control qubit in |+>. So essentially what you are doing here is finding out what does CNOT do when the control qubit is |+>. Since we know what CNOT does when the control qubit is |0> or |1> we can calculate what it does when the control qubit is |+> if we write out the tensor product.
